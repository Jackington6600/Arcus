Marksman

A physcial class based around long ranged single and multi-target damage and shot calling.
Suggested attributes: Perception or Dexterity.

Level Name Description Target AP Cost
Core Ranger You are accurate even at the greatest ranges.

For each level in Marksman, increase the range of all short range weapons by 5ft (max bonus 50ft) and all long range weapons by 10ft (max bonus 100ft).

Self Pass
1 Aim! Command your allies to take aim at the weak points.

Mark a creature within 100ft. All attacks against the marked creature also add 2 + your PER modifier to attack rolls.

A+E, 100ft, Single target 1
1 Point Blank Shot Push back your enemies with a projectile of great force.

Make a ranged weapon attack against a creature within 10ft ignoring any disadvantage due to being within 5ft. On a hit the creature is pushed 20ft directly away from you. If this push movement is stopped by another creature or solid object, the initial target suffers additional Light damage and is knocked prone. 2 FORT points to resist the push, 1 FORT point to resist the prone.

A+E, 10ft (Ranged weapon required), Single target 2
1 Pinning Shot Lock your target in place with pinning fire.

Make a ranged weapon attack against a creature. On a hit the creature is immobilised and hobbled. If you successfully immobilise the target, and there is an ally within melee range, the ally may spend their reaction to deal Medium damage the initial target. 2 FORT points to resist the immobilise, the hobbled cannot be resisted.

A+E, Weapon (Ranged weapon required), Single target 2
1 Advance! Command your allies to press the attack.

All allies within 50ft may immediately spend their reaction to move up to 40ft closer to a target within 100ft. They must take the shortest path possible - avoiding any intentional self harm/endangerment.

A, 50ft/100ft, Single target 1
1 Expeditious Retreat Move away from engagements with flawless grace.

Attacks of opportunity have DisAdv (--) against you.

Self Pass
2 Reactive Retreat You can escape danger better than most.

If you are hit and damaged by a melee attack, you may spend your reaction to immediately move up to 10ft.

Self, Melee React
2 Piercing Shot Strike through foes with a piercing projectile.

Make a ranged weapon attack against all creatures in a 30ft line originating from you. This attack ignores any disadvantage due to being within 5ft.

A+E, All within 40ft straight line from self (AoE) (Ranged weapon required) 2
2 Suppressive Fire Suppress the hostile ranged offensive.

Select a point within 100ft and create a 15ft radius zone. All enemies within this zone have DisAdv (--) on all ranged attack rolls. This DisAdv cannot be resisted but a creature in the zone may suffer Light damage to ignore the DisAdv (--). If creatures leave the area the DisAdv (--) effect ends.

E, 100ft (Ranged weapon required), All enemies within a 15ft radius (AoE) 1
2 Fire! Command your allies to open fire.

Select a creature within 100ft and mark it. Each time the marked target suffers damage, it also suffers your PER modifier as additional damage. A creature can only be marked by one "Fire!" ability at a time.

A+E, 100ft, Single target 2
3 Ricochet Bounce a deadly projectile between foes.

Make a ranged weapon attack against a creature. On a hit you may select a second creature within 15ft of the initial target, and a third creature within 15ft of the second. The second and third creatures both suffer half your weapon damage (e.g. (Medium+PER)/2).

A+E, Weapon (Ranged weapon required), 3 targets 2
3 Long Shot The greater the range, the more devastating the shot.

Make a ranged weapon attack against a creature. On a hit deal an additional Light damage for every 20ft past 50ft the creature is away from you (up to a maximum of 5 x Light additional damage at 150ft). For example, 95ft deals an additional 2 x Light damage, and 60ft would result in no additional damage.

A+E, Weapon (Ranged weapon required), Single target 2
3 Opportunist Seize every opportunity to strike.

When an ally within weapon range makes an attack of opportunity against a creature, you may spend your reaction to make a ranged weapon attack against the same creature.

A+E, Weapon (Ranged weapon required), Single target React
3 In Position Lay prone for greater stability and accuracy.

You now gain Adv (+) instead of DisAdv (-) while attacking with ranged weapons when prone. This does not include thrown weapon attacks.

Self Pass
3 Bear’s Fortitude Your fortitude resembles that of a bear.

Gain an additional 5 FORT resistance points.

Self Pass
3 Cat’s Reflexes Your reflexes resemble that of a cat.

Gain an additional 5 REFL resistance points.

Self Pass
3 Bull’s Will Your will power resembles that of a bull.

Gain an additional 5 WILL resistance points.

Self Pass
4 Back Up! Call upon your allies for reinforcement.

Select an ally within 50ft. This ally may use up to 75ft of regular movement to reach an unoccupied point within 5ft of you. They must take the shortest path possible - avoiding any intentional self harm/endangerment.

A+E, 50ft, Single target 1
4 Running Shot Rapidly reposition and open fire.

Move up to 75ft and make a ranged weapon attack against a creature. This movement may be broken up by the attack. On a hit the creature is hobbled. 2 FORT points to resist the hobble.

A+E, Weapon (Ranged weapon required), Single target 3
4 Alert Your sensitive senses never fail you.

Gain a +5 bonus to your initiative rolls. Any levels of DisAdv you have on attack rolls from not being able to see your target is reduced by 1 level (e.g. from (---) to (--)). You still require line of sight.

Self Pass
5 Rapid Shot Unleash a rapid close range volley.

Make a ranged weapon attack against up to 3 creatures within 15ft. The same creature cannot be hit twice.

A+E, 15ft (Ranged weapon required), 3 targets 2
5 Covering Fire You’ve got your friends covered.

When an ally within weapon range provokes an attack of opportunity, use your reaction to impose DisAdv (--) on the attack.

A+E, Weapon (Ranged weapon required), Single target React
5 Demoralise Demoralise a foe and extinguish their strength.

Select a creature within 100ft and mark it. All damage on their next attack or ability is halved. 2 WILL points to resist the damage reduction.

A+E, 100ft, Single target 1
6 Commanding Shout Command your allies to seize the advantage.

Select an ally within 50ft. This ally may spend their reaction to move up to 25ft in a way of their choice, or make a melee weapon attack. You also cannot use your reaction this turn.

A, 50ft, Single target 1
6 Overwatch Stand vigilant over a protected area.

Select a point within weapon range and create a 5ft radius zone. You may make a ranged weapon attack against creatures of your choice that enter or end their turn within this zone. The same creature cannot be attacked twice in one turn and you must have line of sight to the creature when attacking.

A+E, Weapon (Ranged weapon required), Single target 2
7 Clear Focus Your deft focus grants you deadly accuracy.

Gain Adv (+) on all attacks this turn if you did not suffer any damage since the end of your last turn. If you suffer any damage during your turn this effect ends.

Self Pass
7 Barrage Unleash a barrage of falling projectiles.

Select a point within weapon range. All creatures within a 15ft radius suffer Light+(DEX or PER) damage.

All, Weapon (Ranged weapon required), All within 20ft radius (AoE) 2
8 Power Draw Sacrifice accuracy for immense power.

You may impose DisAdv (-) on your next single target ranged weapon attack and add Light bonus damage if it hits. You may impose DisAdv (--) to add Medium bonus damage instead. You may impose DisAdv (---) to add Heavy bonus damage instead.

A+E, Weapon (Ranged weapon required), Single target Pass
8 Relentless Strike Press the offence as your foes fall.

When you kill a creature, you may spend your reaction to make a ranged weapon attack against another creature.

A+E, Weapon (Ranged weapon required), Single React
9 Ranged Duelist Return fire and assert your ranged dominance.

When you are hit by ranged attack you may spend your reaction to make a ranged weapon attack against the source of the attack.

A+E, Weapon (Ranged weapon required), Single target React
9 Achilles’ Heel Brand a target with a new weakness.

Select a creature within 100ft, mark it, and choose one resistance point type (e.g. REFL). The next time the creature chooses to spend this resistance point type it suffers Heavy damage. This does not include resistance point drain.

A+E, 100ft, Single Target 1
10 Close Quarters Specialist Your attacks at close range are exceptionally lethal.

You no longer have DisAdv (-) for ranged attacks when there is a hostile creature within 5ft. You also gain Adv (+) on all ranged attacks made against a creature within 30ft.

Self, 5ft/30ft Pass
10 Sharpshot Make the impossible shot.

Your next single target attack ranged roll gains Adv (+++).

Self 0
10 Leading Strike Strike first, strike hard.

Make a ranged weapon attack against a creature with full health. You deal additional damage equal to your DEX or PER value (not Modifier).

A+E, Weapon (Ranged weapon required), Single target 2
