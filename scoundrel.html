Scoundrel

A physcial class based around versatile high single target damage, mobility, and inflicting conditions.
Suggested attributes: Strength, Dexterity, or Perception.

Level Name Description Target AP Cost
Core Manoeuvrable You know how to be in the right place at the right time.

You gain 5ft of free movement each turn. After reaching level 3 in Scoundrel this increases to 10ft, and at level 6 it increases again to 15ft.

Self Pass
1 Single Out Pick off the stragglers and the weak.

Make a weapon attack against a creature and on a hit deal additional Medium damage. This additional damage is reduced by 1 for each other creature within a 10ft radius of the initial target (this excludes you and the initial target). This additional damage cannot drop below 0.

A+E, Weapon 2
1 Entangling Manoeuvre Tangle your foe in rope as you move nimbly around them.

Immobilise a creature within 5ft of you, then move yourself to any position within 15ft of that same creature. 2 REFL points to resist the immobilise.

A+E, 5ft, Single target 1
1 Flashbang Dazzle your foes with a small explosive.

Select a point within 50ft and blind all creatures in a 10ft radius. 1 REFL point to resist the blind.

All, 50ft, All within 10ft radius of target tile (AoE) 1
1 Expose Weakness Reveal your foe’s greatest strengths and weaknesses.

Make a weapon attack against a creature. On a hit the creature loses 3 resistance points of your choice. For each resistance type reduced from 1 to 0, it suffers additional Light damage. You also learn all of the creature’s vulnerabilities, weaknesses, resistances, and immunities.

A+E, Weapon, Single target 2
1 Expeditious Retreat Move away from engagements with flawless grace.

Attacks of opportunity have DisAdv (--) against you.

Self Pass
2 Poisoned Strike Coat your weapon in a sickening poison.

Make a weapon attack against a creature. On a hit all damage dealt is poison damage and it ignores all damage resistances and immunities. For each resistance type that the creature has no remaining resistance points, it suffers additional Light poison damage.

A+E, Weapon, Single target 2
2 Reactive Retreat You can escape danger better than most.

If you are hit and damaged by a melee attack, you may spend your reaction to immediately move up to 10ft.

Self, Melee React
2 Hit and Run You have an essential skill for any effective skirmisher.

Move up to 40ft and at the end of your turn move back to where you were when this ability was used.

Self 1
2 Smoke Bomb Conceal an area with thick smoke.

Select a point within 50ft, all tiles within 5ft of this point become filled with smoke. All creatures in this area are blinded and invisible. The cloud lasts for 1 round as normal and the effects cannot be resisted. Leaving the area of smoke immediately removes the blinded and invisible conditions. The area of smoke also blocks line of sight.

All, 50ft, All within 5ft radius of target tile (AoE) 1
3 Adrenaline A surge of adrenaline pushes you to new limits, but comes at a cost.

Gain 1 additional AP this turn, but next turn you start with 2 fewer AP.

Self 0
3 Shadow Step Move with unparalleled stealth and haste.

Move 40ft without breaking hidden or invisibility. At the end of this movement you may attempt the Hide core ability at no additional AP cost.

Self 1
3 Surprise Attack Execute the perfect critical strike against a defenceless target.

Make an auto hit weapon attack against a creature. This attack is a critical hit if any one of the following conditions are met: 1) You are invisible or hidden from the creature. 2) If the cureature has any of the following conditions: blind, prone, immobilised, stunned, restrained, incapacitated, paralysed or unconscious.

A+E, Weapon, Single target 2
3 Headshot Daze your target with a well timed blow to the head.

Make a weapon attack against a creature with Adv (++). On a hit the creature becomes stunned. 2 FORT points to resist the stun.

A+E, Weapon, Single target 2
3 Bear’s Fortitude Your fortitude resembles that of a bear.

Gain an additional 5 FORT resistance points.

Self Pass
3 Cat’s Reflexes Your reflexes resemble that of a cat.

Gain an additional 5 REFL resistance points.

Self Pass
3 Bull’s Will Your will power resembles that of a bull.

Gain an additional 5 WILL resistance points.

Self Pass
4 Unmatched Precision Strike with perfect accuracy.

Whenever you make an attack you may choose to give the attack the auto hit property. This effect lasts until the end of your next turn.

Self 0
4 Stalk Prowl the battlefield and hunt your prey.

After you make an attack of opportunity, you can also instantly move up to 25ft. Also as a reaction when a creature within 5ft of you dies you may instantly move up to 25ft.

E, Melee, Single Target React
4 Exploit Conditions Your foes will suffer for their afflictions.

Make a weapon attack against a creature and on a hit deal additional Light damage for each negative condition it currently has.

A+E, Weapon, Single target 2
5 Cloak and Dagger Lash out and elude your foes, staying mobile all the while.

If you have moved within 5ft of a hostile creature during your turn, deal Medium damage to that creature within 5ft. That creature cannot make attacks of opportunity against you this round.

A+E, 5ft, Single target 1
5 Punish Someone has made themselves a susceptible target. Punish them.

Select a creature within 5ft. If the creature has any of the following conditions in this list (hobbled, immobilised, prone, blind, stunned, silenced) you may inflict them with one other condition in the list of your choice. 2 REFL points to resist the chosen condition.

A+E, 5ft, Single target 1
5 Rupture Tendons Each step they take will be agony.

Make a weapon attack against a creature and on a hit the creature is marked. While marked the creature suffers Light damage for each tile (5ft) moved. Must be voluntary movement and does not include teleportation.

A+E, Weapon, Single target 2
6 Break Free No one can keep you where you don’t want to be.

When you are inflicted with any of the following conditions: hobbled, prone, immobilised, and/or incapacitated, remove all of the above conditions and immediately jump to a point you can see within 20ft. You also become immune to these conditions until the start of your next turn.

Self 1
6 Disable Diminish their powers.

Silence a creature within 5ft. 2 REFL points to resist the silence.

A+E, 5ft, Single target 1
7 Paralysing Venom Paralyse your victim with a deadly toxin.

Make a weapon attack against a creature and on a hit paralyse them. 2 FORT points to resist the paralysis.

A+E, Weapon, Single target 2
7 Evasive Stance You can dodge anything that comes your way.

You gain a +5 bonus to AC and may spend unlimited REFL resistance points without changing your current total. This AC bonus stacks with any other bonuses.

Self 1
8 Riposte Lash back when they least expect it.

When you are damaged by a melee attack, you may spend your reaction to stun the enemy. 2 FORT points to resist the stun.

A+E, 5ft, Single target React
8 Thousand Strikes Dash and dice in a relentless flurry.

Move up to 75ft and you may choose to inflict Light damage to any creature you move within 5ft of during this movement only. The same creature cannot be hit twice.

A+E, 5ft, All within 5ft of 75ft movement (AoE) 3
9 Exploit Weakness Every weakness shall be exploited indiscriminately and harshly.

Make a weapon attack against a creature. On a hit: if it has 0 FORT points it is knocked prone. If it has 0 REFL points it becomes stunned. If it has 0 WILL points it becomes frightened of you. None or all 3 conditions can be applied.

A+E, Weapon, Single target 2
9 Focus senses Always be alert and ready for the next conflict.

When you enter combat and roll initiative, if you have less than 4 REFL points, set your REFL points to 4. Your REFL points cannot be set to more than your maximum.

Self Pass

You can spend 2 REFL points as 1 FORT point.

Self Pass
10 Assassinate Your foe barely clings to life. Finish them.

Make a weapon attack against a creature. On a hit and if the creature is below half its HP maximum, you deal additional damage equal to your DEX or PER value (not Modifier).
